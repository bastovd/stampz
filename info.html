ABOUT LOVESTAMP

LoveStamp is currently in Alpha testing. Expect regular changes and updates.

LoveStamp provides free digital stamps for everyone. As e-mail messaging is gradually replacing regular mail, our mission is to recreate the culture of stamp collecting in e-mail clients. We have launched a test version of the service currently compatible with the GMail web client. LoveStamp will strive to provide best stamp designs and stamp collecting experience for important events in our users' lives.

GET LOVESTAMP

LoveStamp is currently only available for Gmail web client.

To use LoveStamp in your Gmail client, download and install the latest version of the
LoveStamp Chrome Extension
1. When you first compose a new letter, click on the LoveStamp icon in the compose window task bar.
2. Sign Up to LoveStamp.
3. Now when you click the icon again a window with all available stamps will pop-up.
4. You can always get more stamps from the official website.
